About Us

Merakishilp is the fusion of two words MERAKI means when you leave a piece of yourself in your work and SHILP, the craft.

Merakishilp genuinely believes in promoting sustainable, authentic, classic, timeless, and GI (Geographical Indication based) collectionof craftsmanship of Indian Artisan. Our vision is truly resonates with rich culture of India.

MerakiShilp is a platform where we bring handcrafted pieces of different craftsman across Indian States.These handcrafted products never lose their charm and always make a place on runways.

My love for weaves and Handicraft products inspires me to become from an avid admirer to promoter.Find therich and diverse heritage of Indian handicraft products which are handpicked from across India here. With every purchase at MerakiShilp you are contributing to the bigger cause from supporting and empowering weavers to preserve our Traditional art.
